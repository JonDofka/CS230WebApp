Upon completion of our Cosmetology Program students will be prepared to take the State Board Examination to become a licensed West Virginia Cosmetologist

Coursework includes:

Hair Cutting

Hairstyling

Hair Coloring

Permanent Weaving

Basic Manicuring & Pedicuring

Facials

Anatomy & Physiology

Cosmetology Distance Learning

Distance Learning students will enjoy the same personalized learning experience through virtual theory and in-person practical instruction.

Rotation will include two weeks online + one week on campus until all theory is completed, then the student will be required to be on campus every day through the end of the program.
