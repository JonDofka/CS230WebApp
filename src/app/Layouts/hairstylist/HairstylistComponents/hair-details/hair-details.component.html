Earn Your West Virginia Hairstylist License

Upon completion of Morgantown Beauty College’s Hairstylist Program students will be fully prepared to become a licensed West Virginia Hairstylist.

What Does The Hairstylist Program Class Schedule Look Like?

The Hairstylist Program includes classes scheduled from Tuesdays through Saturdays from 8:30 AM – 4:30 PM totalling 1000 hours of coursework.

If your busy lifestyle doesn’t permit you to attend our normal class schedule don’t let that stop you from applying! We will be happy to assist with accommodating your schedule, just stop in and talk with us or feel free to call us at 304.292.8475 Tuesday through Thursday.

Coursework includes:

General Hairdressing

Choosing The Right Haircut

Proper Use Of Hairstyling Tools & Equipment

Hair Treatments

Hair Coloring

Practical Salon/Barbershop Experience

Hairstylist Distance Learning

Distance Learning students will enjoy the same personalized learning experience through virtual theory and in-person practical instruction.

Rotation will include two weeks online + one week on campus until all theory is completed, then the student will be required to be on campus every day through the end of the program.
