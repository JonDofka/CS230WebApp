What To Expect From Your Esthetic Program Class Schedule

A Dermalogica Partner School, the Esthetics Program focuses on skin care by teaching different types of facials, microdermabrasion, skin mapping, product knowledge, full body waxing, and makeup application.

Classes are scheduled from Tuesdays through Saturdays meeting from 8:30 AM – 3:00 PM totaling 600 hours of coursework. If you find yourself facing scheduling conflicts we are happy to discuss various ways to accommodate your time. Please just call us directly at 304.292.8475 or stop by the college and we will work with you.

Once you’ve completed Morgantown Beauty College’s Esthetics Program you will be ready to take the State Board Examination to become a licensed West Virginia Esthetician.

Coursework includes:

Basic & Advanced Skin Care

Makeup

Facial Massage

Nutrition For Health & Beauty

Health of Skin

Skin Structure

Temporary Hair Removal

Esthetic Distance Learning

Distance Learning students will enjoy the same personalized learning experience through virtual theory and in-person practical instruction.

Rotation will include one week online + one week on campus until all theory is completed, then the student will be required to be on campus every day through the end of the program.
